Our Purpose

In the meantime, there is a need of computerization for mechanizing the errand of tracking down the specific individual by perceiving specific picture and contrasting that picture and other picture to check regardless of whether the two pictures has same qualities. By doing this we will come to know whether the missing individual in the picture clicked from specific area is right or not, and on the off chance that it is right then police can begin their subsequent stages to track down the individual from that area.
